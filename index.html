Rats Vs. Wall Street

Rats

For a long time, I feel like rats have gotten a bad wrap. They're always looked down on as the gross disease carrying sewer dwellers, when in reality, they're incredibly smart ressourceful little dudes. Their circumstances have just been slightly more unfortuate than other animals. Rats didn't spread the plague, bacteria ridden fleas did. And they just so happen to choose rats to be their victims. So I plan to clear up public perception on them by clearing up some misinformation, and by showing how important rats really are, so why not click here

Wall Street

For a long time, the real slimy sewer dwelling vermin roamed around completely unnoticed, dressed in fancy clothing and drowning in money. Yet no one ever looks twice at the scuminess of wall street. So I thought it'd be funny to point and laugh at the many ridiculous fails at a business that the rich suffer from so often.
